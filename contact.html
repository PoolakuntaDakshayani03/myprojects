Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for internships through your platform?

Create a student account on our website, complete your profile, and browse available internships. When you find opportunities that match your interests, click "Apply" and follow the application instructions.

What should I do if I'm having technical issues with my application?

Please contact our technical support team at support@internconnect.com or call (415) 555-1234. Be sure to include screenshots of any error messages you're receiving.

How can my company list internship opportunities on your site?

Companies should register for an employer account and complete the verification process. Once approved, you can post internship opportunities through your employer dashboard. For large organizations needing multiple postings, contact our employer services team.

Is there a fee for students to use your platform?

No, our platform is completely free for students to search and apply for internships. We generate revenue through employer partnerships and premium services for companies.
